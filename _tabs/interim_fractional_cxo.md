## Interim/Fractional CXO

Scale-ups face a unique challenge: they need C-level expertise to navigate critical growth phases, but may not need or be able to afford a full-time executive. I provide experienced, hands-on leadership to help companies bridge that gap.

### When to Engage

**Ideal Scenarios for Interim/Fractional Executives:**
- **Growth Transition**: Scaling from startup to scale-up (Series A-C)
- **Executive Search**: Bridging the gap while recruiting permanent leadership
- **Transformation**: Navigating strategic pivots or major change initiatives
- **Crisis Management**: Addressing urgent operational or financial challenges
- **Special Projects**: Leading complex initiatives (M&A, fundraising, restructuring)

### My Approach

**1. Rapid Onboarding (Weeks 1-2)**
- Immerse in company operations, culture, and metrics
- Assess strengths, gaps, and immediate priorities
- Align with board and leadership team on objectives

**2. Strategic Execution (Months 1-6)**
- Implement quick wins to build momentum
- Execute long-term strategic initiatives
- Build and empower teams for sustainable success
- Establish systems and processes for scale

**3. Transition Planning (Final Phase)**
- Prepare organization for permanent leadership or independence
- Document processes and knowledge transfer
- Support recruitment and onboarding of successors
